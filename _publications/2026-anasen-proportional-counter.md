This manuscript describes the conceptualization and implementation of a novel design upgrade for the internal tracking proportional counter within the **ANASEN (Array for Nuclear Astrophysics and Structure with Exotic Nuclei)** active target.

### Technical Highlights:
* **Design Innovation:** Introduction of a new internal tracking geometry to improve vertex reconstruction and background rejection.
* **Performance Metrics:** Documented an order-of-magnitude improvement in position resolution during alpha-source calibration.
* **Experimental Validation:** Details the performance of the upgraded detector during experimental campaigns investigating $^{27}Al(\alpha, p)$, $^{24}Mg(\alpha, p)$, and $^{17}F(\alpha, p)$ reactions at the John D. Fox Laboratory.
* **DAQ Integration:** Discusses the synchronization of digital signal processing with the proportional counter's timing response for high-rate experiments.

This work serves as a foundational technical reference for future $(\alpha, p)$ reaction studies using the upgraded ANASEN system.
