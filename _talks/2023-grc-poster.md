Presented a poster detailing the design and calibration of the upgraded internal tracking proportional counter for the ANASEN active target. 

### Key Highlights:
* Discussed the integration of CAEN digitizers for digital DAQ.
* Showcased preliminary alpha-source calibration data demonstrating improved position resolution.
* Outlined the experimental goals for upcoming $(\alpha, p)$ reaction studies using radioactive ion beams.
