Oral presentation discussing the experimental results of the $^{27}Al(\alpha, p)$ reaction study performed at the John D. Fox Laboratory.

### Presentation Highlights:
* **Detector Performance:** Demonstrated the successful implementation of the upgraded internal tracking proportional counter.
* **Resolution Improvements:** Discussed the order-of-magnitude improvement in position resolution achieved during the experimental campaign.
* **Data Analysis:** Presented preliminary cross-section results and the impact of the upgraded tracking on background rejection.
* **Experimental Setup:** Detailed the integration of the CAEN-based digital DAQ for coincident data readout.
