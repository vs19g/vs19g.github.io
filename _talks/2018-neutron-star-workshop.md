Attended a specialized workshop focused on the physics of compact objects, specifically the structure, evolution, and observational signatures of neutron stars.

### Workshop Focus:
* **Equation of State (EoS):** Explored the theoretical models for dense nuclear matter and their constraints from neutron star mass-radius measurements.
* **Astrophysical Observations:** Discussions on pulsar timing, X-ray emissions, and the then-emerging field of multi-messenger astronomy following GW170817.
* **Nuclear Physics Links:** Analyzed the relationship between terrestrial nuclear experiments and the properties of neutron star crusts.
