Participated in an intensive summer school focused on the fundamentals of plasma physics and nuclear fusion, organized by the IPFN and associated with the ITER project.

### Core Topics:
* **Plasma Theory:** Kinetic theory, MHD (Magnetohydrodynamics), and wave propagation in plasmas.
* **Nuclear Fusion:** Magnetic confinement fusion (Tokamaks) and the engineering challenges of the ITER project.
* **Laboratory Experience:** Hands-on sessions involving plasma discharge characterization and diagnostic techniques.
* **Specialized Seminars:** Attended lectures on the latest developments in European fusion research and high-energy density physics.
